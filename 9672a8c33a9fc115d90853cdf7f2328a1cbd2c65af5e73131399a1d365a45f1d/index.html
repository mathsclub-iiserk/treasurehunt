Shipwreck
As the waves roll over your ship, as the water gushes into your room and fills it up, as the stench of blood from your crew engulfs you - you think of the game of matches you played. Maybe if you had done it correctly you wouldn't have argued with Ritam. Maybe it wouldn't have escalated into a fight that caused the fire. Maybe then you could have handled the storm better and saved everyone's lives. But no use thinking of it now: you close your eyes as the water embraces your fragile frame in its arms.
