Q5: Bridges
You come across an age-old stray note, that has surprisingly withstood the sands of time. It reveals a riddle, which might lead you closer to your destination.
Oh traveller from foreign lands! The path to glory is all but fun!
Must you cross the river once, god shall forbid tracing back;
For seven is not lucky, and never shall you return!
But in the middle of it all,
Which thinker rests now, pal?
Lest your answer not be immediate, look up on your map.
What is the last name of the person referred to in this riddle? For example, if you think that the person is John Doe, then your answer should be Doe.
