Q13: Duel
On your journey, you stumble across a cavern. As you start descending carefully, you feel the air around you getting colder. The smell is not the most pleasant, but definitely a mesmerizing one; it talks of runes and pamphlets of lore, and treasures that eluded the hands of many a unfortunate hunter. You move along till you chance upon a headstone, with this riddle etched on it.
Normale, has it been thy friend?
What group, helped thee in the end?
Young chap, should ye look along,
Squinting; can't be closed at all,
Oh! Love, should it hurt us all?
Sparring deadly, in twenty falls.
What is the last name of the person referred to in this riddle? For example, if you think that the person is John Doe, then your answer should be Doe.
