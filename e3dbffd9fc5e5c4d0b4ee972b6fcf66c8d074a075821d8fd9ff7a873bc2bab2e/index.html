Q18: Day
The treasure chest opens with a creak. Everyone gazes at the immaculate beauty of the chest, the intricate designs on its edges. Soon you realize, the chest has two compartments. On the top, there is a riddle - the final one, to unlock what you have wanted for years; no, decades! It's finally time! Will you get this one and bring peace and prosperity for all those who respect you? Will you be richer than Kings around the world? You close your eyes and recall the hidden note you had found from a sunken ship near an abandoned harbour one day - it promised a billion to its finder, in denominations of two. Is it your time at last? You solve the riddle, your eyes gleaming in the absolute insanity of winning the world.
Belittled, your life was hard;
In woodlands, born erode afar,
Dedication, for rayaht mirrored,
When unity, and a dozen words,
Come thrice the price, as far as I heard,
At the end, did ya achieve heaven,
For t'is age is two and twelve elevens.
What is the last name of the person referred to in this riddle? For example, if you think that the person is John Doe, then your answer should be Doe.
